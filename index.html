Welcome to HotCSE 2021

HotCSE 2021, the 1st China Workshop on Hot Topics in Computer Science and Engineering will be held on Feb 04 2021, virtually due to COVID-19. HotCSE is a high-level workshop that provides the opportunities for debating frontier research progress on computer science&engineering such as AI/ML, Systems&Networking, Vision&Graphics and innovative ideas for future research.

HotCSE 2021 welcomes researchers from both academic community and industrial community in China to push forward the development of computer science and engineering, and also participants to interact with them and exchange ideas. HotCSE 2021 contains distinguished keynotes and a fantastic panel, and looks forward to your participation.
